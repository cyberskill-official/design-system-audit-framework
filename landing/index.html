Why us, specifically

We authored the methodology you'd otherwise be hiring someone else to apply.

Most audit shops are someone running a checklist. CyberSkill is the practice that decided what goes on the checklist, why each criterion is weighted the way it is, and what scoring actually rewards. That makes a measurable difference in the gray cases — which, for design systems, is most of them.

Senior consultants on every engagement

Audits are led by senior practitioners with direct authorship of the framework. No handoff to juniors mid-engagement. Every report carries the firm's signature.

Self-tested in public

Our own design system is published as the framework's reference case study at 84.6% (L5 Optimised). The full report is public. The process you'd be paying for is the one you can read in advance.

Calibration, not vibes

Every paid audit ships with a five-criterion human spot-check against the agent's scoring. Anything off by ≥2 points triggers a calibration discussion before we sign. That's the framework's stability guarantee.

Global delivery, Vietnamese cost basis

The methodology is publicly auditable. Delivery is async-first across US, EU, APAC, and AU timezones. The pricing reflects our cost basis, not a Western agency markup.

How a typical audit goes

Email Monday, signed report by week two.

Six steps; nothing dramatic. The ones that go quickly are the ones where we both stop talking and let the work happen.

  1. No. 01

    You email

    One paragraph: what the system is, how big the team, what's hurting. Send to info@cyberskill.world. We answer within 48 hours.

  2. No. 02

    Free 30-min scoping call

    We figure out shape, scope, and whether we're the right fit. Sometimes the right fit is "use the framework yourself." We say so.

  3. No. 03

    Fixed quote in 48 hours

    One number, signable. Mutual NDA if you need one. 50% on signing to commit our calendar, 50% on delivery.

  4. No. 04

    Read access, then we work

    Docs, source, Storybook. We start within a week. Most of the work is async; we'll ping you with questions, not status reports.

  5. No. 05

    Signed report, walkthrough call

    You receive the audit report and a phased improvement plan. We walk your team through it on a 60-minute call — we record it for your archives.

  6. No. 06

    You decide what's next

    Implementation, retainer, or you take it from here. No upsell pressure, no "exclusive partnership" lock-in. The framework is yours; the report is yours.

Common questions

A few things people ask first.

Why pay you when the framework is free?

The framework is the rules of the game. The audit is playing the game well. Teams running their first self-audit usually ship something passable but uncalibrated — they score too high, miss the FIXED versus DYNAMIC distinction, or skip the human spot-check that keeps scoring stable across cycles. We've done it about a hundred times. That's what you're paying for.

How are you different from Big Medium or Brad Frost?

They're excellent. They're also Western-priced, US-time-zone, and don't bring a published framework as their methodology. CyberSkill brings the framework, runs the audit on a Vietnamese cost basis, and does it without compromising on calibration. Pick whoever fits the brief.

What if my team isn't in Vietnam's timezone?

We work async-first. Most communication happens on email and a shared workspace (Notion, Slack, Teams — yours or ours). One or two calls a week as needed. We've worked with US, EU, AU, and APAC teams; timezone has never been the blocker.

Will you publish a case study without permission?

No. Case studies are opt-in. If you opt in we knock $1,000 off the audit and put your logo on the framework's examples/ folder. If you don't, the report stays private; no one sees it but you.

What if the audit doesn't find much?

That's a great outcome — your system is healthier than expected. We don't refund (we still did the work), but we're happy to redirect the engagement into a Tier 2 quote on the priority gaps we did find, or send you on your way with a signed clean bill of health.

How does payment work, practically?

Audits: 50% on signing, 50% on delivery. Builds: 30 / 40 / 30 across milestones. Retainers: monthly. Wire transfer, PayPal, or Stripe; VND for Vietnamese clients. We send invoices in USD by default.

Can we sign a mutual NDA first?

Yes. Either yours or ours. We sign before the scoping call if you'd rather discuss specifics under one.
